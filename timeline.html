Reading Guide

Audit & Exam Cards

The top section shows upcoming audits and exams. Click any card to expand it and see which projects are linked to that audit. The left-side color stripe matches the audit's color used throughout the page.

Project Timeline (Gantt Chart)

Each row represents a project. The horizontal bar shows start-to-end duration. Small circles on the bar are milestones. The colored vertical shaded bands behind the bars indicate audit deadline dates — the taller/wider they are, the more visible they are relative to the project. Click any row to expand details.

Impact Matrix

The bottom table maps projects to audits. A colored circle with an arrow means that project directly impacts that audit. A dash means no connection. Use this to quickly identify which projects carry the most audit exposure.
